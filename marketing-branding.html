Questions

Common questions about marketing & branding

What's the difference between marketing and branding here?

Branding is how your business looks and what it stands for — the consistent identity and message. Marketing is getting that in front of the right people. We work on both together, because a strong brand makes every marketing effort land harder.

Do you run ad campaigns?

Our focus is the foundation — positioning, message, and presence — that makes any marketing work. We'll be straight about what we do and don't take on, and scope it to what will actually move the needle for you.

Can this be done alongside a website or brand identity?

Yes, and it usually should be. Positioning, identity, and your site reinforce each other — doing them together produces a far more consistent and credible result than any one alone.

Do you work with businesses outside Frisco?

Yes — based in Frisco, TX, working across DFW and with clients nationwide.
