The Challenge

The district had data — but no way to see it, trust it, or act on it in time.

The school's existing student information system was built for compliance and record-keeping — not operational visibility. Administrators who needed to monitor student performance, flag at-risk cases, or pull accountability reports had to work through a series of manual exports, spreadsheet merges, and offline analysis before they could see anything useful.

The result was a team that was always working with yesterday's information, making decisions based on incomplete pictures, and spending hours every week on data work that should have been automatic.

Before
  • Student data scattered across multiple systems
  • Reports required manual exports and spreadsheet merging
  • No real-time view of attendance, behavior, or academics
  • At-risk students identified late — or not at all
  • Accountability logs tracked manually or not at all
  • STAAR and TSI data siloed from day-to-day operations
  • No way to see the full picture for a single student
After
  • Single platform connecting all student data sources
  • Live dashboards — no manual assembly required
  • Real-time attendance, absences, tardies, and trends
  • At-risk indicators surfaced automatically
  • Behavior, contacts, and accountability logged in one place
  • STAAR, TSI, and CCMR data visible alongside daily operations
  • Complete student profile accessible in seconds
The Platform

Six integrated modules. One unified experience.

NexaFlow built a purpose-designed student information portal that consolidated the district's fragmented data sources into a single, role-based platform. Every module was built around how administrators and staff actually work — not how a generic SIS expects them to.

  • Campus-wide snapshot at a glance
  • At-risk indicators and alerts
