NexaFlow Digital offers a modern photo booth experience for events that want something cleaner, more branded, and more flexible than a standard rental setup.

How the guest experience works

Whether the event uses the customized app or the Magic Mirror booth, the guest flow stays simple and easy to follow.

01

Step Up to the Booth

Guests use the experience on-site through the customized app or the Magic Mirror booth.

02

Take Photos

The booth guides users through the capture flow in a clean, event-friendly experience.

03

Receive Photos by Email

After the session, guests receive their photo directly by email so they can access it later.

04

Save, Share, or Print

Once delivered, the image can be downloaded, shared, or ordered as a print after the event.

Photo printing after the event

Guests receive their image by email after the booth session, making it easy to come back later, download the photo, and order a print without slowing down the event experience.

Digital delivery with a clear next step

The booth experience is designed around digital delivery first. Once the session is complete, the image is sent directly by email so guests can access it from their phone or inbox after the event.

That creates a simple path from photo delivery to print ordering for anyone who wants a physical copy later.

How guests print their photo

  1. Open the photo delivery email after the booth session.
  2. View or download the delivered image.
  3. Use the print link provided with the photo.
  4. Place a print order for pickup or delivery.
