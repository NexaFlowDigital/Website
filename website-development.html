Questions

Common questions about websites

Do you use templates or page builders?

No. Every site is built from scratch around your business and brand. That's a core principle for us — templates limit what's possible and make your site look like everyone else's.

Will it work on mobile?

Yes, and we design for mobile first, since that's where most visitors will see it. The site is built to look and work correctly on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Will I be able to update it?

We build with that conversation in mind and set up whatever level of editing makes sense for you, then walk your team through it. And we stay available after launch for changes.

Is the site built to show up on Google?

We build a clean SEO foundation — proper structure, tags, and a sitemap — so search engines can find and index your pages. Ranking also depends on content and time, and we'll be straight with you about what to expect.
