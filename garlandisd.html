A large Texas school district needed a custom system to track student accountability data, automate communication, and give administrators real-time visibility into what was happening across their campus — before problems became crises.

The Challenge

Critical accountability data scattered across systems — with no way to see the full picture in time to act.

The district was tracking essential student accountability data — attendance, truancy, loss of credit, STAAR scores, TSI results, CCMR metrics, and more — but that data lived in separate systems and required significant manual effort to pull together. By the time reports were assembled, the window to intervene had often already passed.

Staff were spending hours combining spreadsheet exports, reformatting data, filtering for specific groups, and sending manual notifications. The process was slow, error-prone, and left gaps in visibility that directly affected student outcomes.

Before
  • Reports pulled from multiple disconnected systems
  • Hours of manual spreadsheet combining and filtering
  • Errors introduced during manual data consolidation
  • Notifications sent manually — or not at all
  • No way to see trends or at-risk patterns in real time
  • Data often arrived too late to act on
After
  • Single platform with live data across all accountability areas
  • Dashboards update automatically — no manual assembly
  • Accurate, consolidated data administrators can trust
  • Automated emails to students and families for time missed
  • Interactive dashboards to drill into problems by campus, class, or student
  • Real-time visibility that enables timely intervention
The Solution

Two interconnected tools — built as one platform.

The system was built in two integrated parts: a Student Monitoring System for searching and tracking individual students across accountability metrics, and an Attendance & LOC Reporting Dashboard for campus-wide visibility into absences, tardies, makeup hours, and loss of credit status.

The Result

Hours of manual work eliminated. Accountability data available in real time, for the first time.

What previously required hours of spreadsheet work — and still produced unreliable results — is now a live, accurate platform that campus administrators access daily. The system gave the district the visibility to identify at-risk students earlier, communicate faster, and manage compliance data without the manual overhead that had defined the process before.
