How the experience works

The setup is simple for guests and practical for hosts. The goal is to create a smooth event experience without adding confusion or unnecessary steps.

01

Take Photos at the Event

Guests use the booth during the event to capture photos in a branded digital setup.

02

Receive Photos by Email

Images are delivered digitally so guests can access them from their phone or inbox shortly after use.

03

Save, Share, or Print

Once delivered, guests can download their photo, share it digitally, or place a print order if they want a physical copy.

Photo printing

Because guests receive their images digitally, printing is a natural next step for anyone who wants a physical copy after the event.

Print your photo after delivery

After your image is emailed to you, you can open it, save it, and order prints through a recommended photo printing provider for pickup or delivery.

This keeps the booth experience digital at the event while still giving guests an easy path to printed photos afterward.
